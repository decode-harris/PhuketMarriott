Marriott Services

Plan a luxury escape to the extraordinary Phuket Marriott Resort & Spa, Merlin Beach. This 5-star hotel welcomes families, couples and business travellers to a beach-side retreat.

World Class Suites

Your luxurious guestroom is just steps from the waves. Open your window to the stunning panoramic view of the Andaman Sea. Private balcony provides views of swaying palm trees, blue green water and the calm white sands.

Private Beach Access

Tri Trang Beach is a quiet, secluded place which is ideal for dedicated sun-lovers. The 500-metre north-facing beach is snuggled into the headland just to the south of Patong.

Discover Patong

Patong is a beach resort town on the west coast of Phuket Island, facing the Andaman Sea in the southwest of Thailand. Its sandy, crescent beach is lined with cafes, restaurants and bars.
